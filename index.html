About us

A clean, relaxed neighbourhood cafe with a little matcha-green lift.

The Lounge Cafe and Eatery is the easy Logan Reserve stop for breakfast, brunch, lunch and coffee, with indoor seating, outdoor tables, takeaway, kids options and friendly service.
